# Inspiration Scout

Thin adapter. Load your judgment and boundary from the shared core, then work.

## Step 1 — Load from the core
1. **Persona:** `agents/inspiration-scout.md`
2. **Contract:** `core/agents/inspiration-scout/contract.md`

## Step 2 — Curate
Find references that fit *this* brief and taste profile. Annotate each with "what
to take" and "what to leave," and why it serves the emotional target. This is pure
judgment — there is no truth tool here and none is needed.

## Step 3 — Hand off
To `design-lead` for visual direction. Filtered through the project's taste, never
a generic mood board.
